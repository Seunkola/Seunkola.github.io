About Us

Onyx Hydrocarbons Limited

Onyx Hydrocarbons Limited is an indigenous premier Oil & Gas Servicing firm dedicated to providing the highest standards of Health, Safety, Security and Environmentally friendly Products/Services that drives economic and human progress.

Onyx Hydrocarbons Limited has experienced rapid growth in response to emerging trends in dynamic industries. Since starting operations, we have through the years grown into a dynamic firm, with a global competitive edge, expanding geometrically in every area of Pipeline Solutions and Asset Integrity including but not limited to oil & gas and marine industry. As a company and as individuals, we take great pride in contributing to the communities where we live and work. We also care about the environment and are proud of the many ways in which our employees work to safeguard it. Our persistent efforts to improve on our safe work environment continue to pay off.

Our Project Execution is focused on timely executions, performance excellence, capital stewardship systems and disciplined cost management systems.

Our Mission

Globally recognized for delivering the highest standards in quality, prompt service and reliability with our partners & associates.

Our Vision is to be a Global Asset Integrity Solutions provider, known for its workforce, partnership and excellence in execution.
